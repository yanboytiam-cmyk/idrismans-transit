RoRo Shipping International

It depends on the method of car shipping you choose, if you want to go with Roll on, Roll off then nothing can be left inside the vehicle – all personal items will need to be removed. If you would like to send extra items along with the vehicle then we will need to go for either a 20ft or 40ft container, or if you are going to Europe from one of the main ports then you can make use of our 40ft shared container service.

Roll on, Roll off shipping is specifically designed for the movement of empty vehicles. The shipping line won't accept any responsibility for any items left inside vehicles, nor will they allow us to declare them on any of the Bills of Lading meaning that any item left inside the vehicle technically means you are trying to smuggle undeclared items in through the border.

It completely depends on the conditions of your contract with your finance company, every financial lender is different so the first step will be to contact your current finance company and ask for their terms regarding taking the vehicle out of the country. *Please note that if you do have finance owing on a vehicle at the time of arrival into l'Afrique, then you will NOT be eligible for the import taxes exemption.

Since Anvers is so far away from Paris (approx 100km) we always give you the option to deliver yourself or to have it collected and delivered via tow truck so the decision is yours.

Compliance is a very thorough, one time process that your vehicle has to go through before it can be registered for the first time in Europe. They test a range of safety features on your vehicle – seatbelt restraints, tyre tread, break pads, windscreens, etc. – they strip the inside of the vehicle so that the actual structure of the vehicle can be inspected, and they test the emissions. Once complete you'll be issued with a certificate of compliance and a warrant of fitness.

Roll on Roll off is 3-5 business days after the arrival of the vessel. A dedicated 20ft or 40ft container is 5-8 business days. A shared Container is 7-14 business days. If you choose our compliance included option then allow a further 5 working days extra from the above stated timeframes.

Decontamination of your goods can only be ordered by The competent authorities . The most Basic of Decontamination can be as little as €135.00 however there are other fees that can apply. These charges can easily exceed four of five hundred euros in total depending on what is required.

You'll need to make sure there is no biological matter (plant or animal material, grass clippings, seeds, leaves, animal hairs, bugs etc.) on your vehicle prior to shipping. Please note that paying hundreds of euros for a professional detail won't guarantee a pass from quarantine, so you are better off cleaning the vehicle yourself, focusing on under the vehicle, under the wheel arches and the engine bay.
