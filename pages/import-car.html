How We Ship

Easy, Safe ... And Affordable

Three proven methods to get your vehicle to its destination — choose the option that fits your timeline and budget.

Roll On Roll Off (RoRo)

Your vehicle is driven directly onto the vessel and secured for the voyage. This is the most cost-effective and commonly used shipping method for standard vehicles. Vehicle must be empty of all personal belongings.

Shared Container (LCL)

Your vehicle shares a 40ft container with other vehicles, significantly reducing costs. Excellent weather protection. Personal items may be placed inside the vehicle but are shipped at owner's risk.

Full Container (FCL)

Your vehicle has an entire container to itself. Maximum protection and security — ideal for classic, luxury or high-value vehicles. You may also pack the container with household goods alongside your vehicle.

Modern Day Shipping Is Very Safe

The international vehicle shipping industry has invested heavily in safety systems, vessel design and vehicle restraint technology. Millions of vehicles are shipped safely every year without incident.

All vessels used by IdrisMans Transit are operated by reputable, internationally-certified shipping lines. Your vehicle is checked in, photographed, and a condition report is generated at origin — providing you with documented proof of its state before it boards the vessel.

We also offer marine cargo insurance for complete peace of mind. Our team will advise on the most appropriate level of cover for your vehicle's value.
