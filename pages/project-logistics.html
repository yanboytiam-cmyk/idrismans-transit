Project Logistics & Management

IdrisMans Transit specialises in project logistics for complex, oversized, and high-value cargo shipments. Our project team has experience managing large-scale logistics for mining, construction, energy, and industrial sectors.

From breakbulk and heavy lift to vessel chartering and multi-modal solutions, we provide end-to-end project logistics management with dedicated project managers who oversee every aspect of your cargo journey.
