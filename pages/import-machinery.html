Common Questions And Responses

We can ship all types of machinery including construction equipment, agricultural machinery, industrial plant, mining equipment, generators, cranes, and specialised manufacturing equipment. Our team has experience with both standard and oversized cargo.

Yes, we specialise in oversized and out-of-gauge cargo. We have access to flat rack containers, open top containers, and breakbulk shipping options for machinery that exceeds standard container dimensions.

Our experienced customs brokerage team handles all import and export documentation, tariff classifications, and customs clearance procedures. We ensure your machinery complies with all relevant l'Internationaln or destination country regulations.
