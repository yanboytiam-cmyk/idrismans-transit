Three Shipping Scenarios We Cover

Moving to l'Afrique

Relocating? We handle full relocation logistics including all paperwork, compliance, and delivery to your new address.

Importing for Resale

Dealership stock shipping with compliance management. We understand the commercial requirements for resale imports.

Temporary Import

Short-term visits where the caravan returns to international. We manage temporary import permits and return logistics.

Pre-Shipping Requirements

Before your caravan can be shipped, certain preparation is required to ensure it passes biosecurity and customs checks:

  • Caravan must be spotless inside and out to pass biosecurity checks
  • Gas bottles and fire extinguishers must be removed
  • Personal belongings and food items are prohibited
  • Current registration documents required

Your caravan will need a contrôle technique , a Gas Safety Certificate (if applicable), and road registration before it can be used in l'Afrique. Our team will guide you through all requirements.

A typical quote includes pickup, delivery, shipping, port fees, customs, and quarantine clearance. Additional potential costs include: contrôle technique and gas certificates (~€300), registration, optional transit insurance (from ~€455), and 15% import taxes unless you qualify for an exemption.
