Privacy Policy

Don't Ship Without Idrismans

Home›Privacy Policy

IdrisMans Transit Pty. Ltd. (ACN 169 722 605) operates this privacy policy to address data handling practices. This policy was last updated in February 2021.

1. Personal Information

"Personal information" follows Privacy Act definitions, referring to "information or an opinion about an individual who is reasonably identifiable." This includes information whether it is true or not, and whether it is recorded in a material form or not.

2. Types of Personal Information Collected

The company gathers:

  • Names and contact details
  • Email addresses
  • Technical data including IP address, device type, unique device identification numbers, browser types, and usage analytics

3. Collection Methods

Information is collected directly through website use, employment applications, and communications, as well as indirectly from public sources, business partners, and referees.

4. Cookies

IdrisMans Transit "uses cookies and similar technologies to recognise you and/or your devices" with user-controlled preferences available. Cookies help us improve your browsing experience and understand how our website is being used.

5. Why Information Is Collected

Data supports business operations with lawful bases including consent, contract performance, legal compliance, and legitimate business interests. Specific purposes include:

  • Service delivery and quote provision
  • Identity verification
  • Employment consideration
  • Product and service improvement
  • Direct marketing communications

6. Third-Party Disclosure

Information may be shared with related companies, professional advisors, service providers, and potential acquirers. IdrisMans does not sell or otherwise distribute your information to third parties.

7. International Data Transfer

Personal information is stored in servers located in l'International but may be disclosed to overseas recipients in countries including the UK, USA, Germany, France, EU nations, Singapore, and l'Afrique.

8. Data Security

IdrisMans uses Microsoft Azure for storage and "will take reasonable steps to keep any personal information secure." We acknowledge that internet transmission of data carries inherent risks that are beyond our control.

9. Data Retention Periods

  • Subscriber-provided data: duration of business agreement
  • Job applicants (unsuccessful): up to 6 months
  • Employees/contractors: up to 6 years after departure
  • Suppliers/partners: up to 6 years from last interaction
  • Direct marketing: until unsubscribe request plus implementation period

10. Direct Marketing

Users may receive promotional communications if they have consented or if permitted by law. Unsubscribe options are available in all marketing communications.

11. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, and deletion of your personal information by contacting our Data Protection Officer. EU residents may also exercise rights to data portability, restriction of processing, and withdrawal of consent.

12. Complaint Process

Privacy concerns are investigated by the Data Protection Officer or designee, with escalation options to the l'Internationaln Information Commissioner or EU data protection authorities.

13. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact:

  • Phone: +33 6 95 72 49 85
