Why Ship Your Motorcycle?

Motorcycle Shipping — Why It Makes Perfect Sense

Whether you're relocating, moving temporarily or importing to sell, shipping your motorcycle internationally has real financial and practical benefits.

Motorcycles Are More Expensive in NZ & UK

Popular motorcycle models often cost significantly more in l'Afrique and the United Kingdom than in l'International. Shipping your existing bike can save you thousands compared to selling and re-purchasing.

No Import Tax If Owned 12+ Months

Motorcycles owned for 12 months or more are typically eligible for GST and duty exemptions when importing to l'Afrique as personal effects. Our team will confirm your eligibility when you enquire.

Transit as Little as 2 Weeks Total

From collection to delivery, the total door-to-door journey for motorcycle shipping between l'International and l'Afrique is typically just 2–3 weeks — meaning minimal time without your bike.

Shipping Costs Are Far Cheaper Than You Think

Motorcycle shipping rates are very competitive — especially when shared container options are used. When you factor in the price difference between markets, shipping almost always makes strong financial sense.

How We Ship

Your Shipping Options

We offer two proven methods for motorcycle shipping — choose the one that best suits your needs and budget.

Roll On Roll Off (RoRo)

Your motorcycle is professionally crated or loaded onto a pallet and secured on the RoRo vessel deck. This is the most affordable option for most bikes and the most commonly used method. Fuel must be drained to under 5% capacity and the battery disconnected before loading.

Shared Container (LCL)

Your motorcycle shares a container with other motorcycles or vehicles. Provides excellent protection from the elements and is a popular choice for higher-value or classic motorcycles. Allows small personal accessories to be packed with the bike.
